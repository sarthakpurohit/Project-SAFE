SAFE

Signal Alert For Emergency

What we do!

On May 24, 2019 a short circuit caused the fire in a coaching complex which took the life of about 15 children, and left many injured. The Surat Fire incident. Those lives may have been saved, were it not for the traffic that obstructed the proper help from reaching in time. What we plan to do is create a system which warns the users of a sudden disaster and suggests them to take an alternate route. Thus preventing a traffic jam around the location and aiding in faster rescue and disaster Mitigation response. At the same time, it protects the user from getting trapped in such a calamity.

How we do it!

It all starts with the user registration on our portal. The user fills a basic form and provides us with his contact details. If something unfortunate occurs, the traffic cameras around this locality focus on taking snaps of the number plate of the vehicles. These snaps are then used to distinguish emergency and non emergency vehicles. The numbers from the civilian vehicle number plates are extracted. If we spot any of our beloved clients heading towards such a danger we immediately send them a message, or a mail to help them re-route their vehicles.

Certain disasters cannot be predicted and no number of precautions can help us mitigate them, if we're too slow to react. Our mission is to create a platform which helps people find a way to decrease the response time and save more lives.
